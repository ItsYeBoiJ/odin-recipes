This broccoli and cauliflower casserole is always a hit!

The croutons on top give it that crunchy dimension that everyone will love.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups broccoli
  • 2 cups cauliflower
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • 1 (15 ounce) package seasoned croutons, crushed
